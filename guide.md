# Stardew Valley Guide
Seeds:
422326280
422323422
Objectives: 
1. Do not sell: Diamond, Ruby and mainly Jade (trade for items in Desert), unless it's the first 3 days and it helps reach a daily goal, do NOT sell Jade at all
1. Mines: level 90 for Sword
1. Level 3 Backpack
1. Community Center 4 bundles + 42500 to get Bus fixed (options below)
    * Spring Crops (Parsnip, Cauliflower, Potato, Green bean)
    *  Spring Foraging
    *  Construction (Wood x 99, Wood x 99, Stone x 99, Hardwood x 10)
    *  Blacksmith (Copper, Iron, Gold bars)
    *  Geologist (Quartz, Earth Crystal, Frozen Tear, Fire Quartz)
    *  Adventurer (Slime x99, Bat Wing x10, Solar Essence, Void Essence)
1. Get 100 bombs and food to run Skull Cavern in Days 15~17 in the Desert Festival
Notes:
1. If you level up a skill your energy will be fully restored by night
### Day 1
1. **GOAL** Repair Bridge in the Beach, energy will be the main issue, so use all items possible to do it
1. Plant 1 Parsnip
1. Chop 9 trees for Foraging lv2
1. Craft a chest
1. Cindersap Forest/Town/Robin House: forage food on the map
1. Town:
    * Buy 1 Cauliflower Seed (plant it at the end of the day)
    * Donate artifact (skip if none was found)
1. Chop trees up to 300, avoid Oak is possible. Do not cut stumps.
1. Beach: repair bridge and forage (Sea Urchin will be required for the Bait Maker)
1. Sell everything except
    *  Stone
    *  Wood
    *  Coal 
    *  20 Fiber
    *  Spring Onions
    *  1 Sap
1. Cut trees until passing out
### Day 2
1. **GOAL** have sellables worth around 2k
1. Craft a Chest and mine for 25 stone if time permits
1. Take 
1. Beach
    * Get Fishing Rod with Willy and fish until his store is open
    * Buy Training Rod and fish on lowest range for most xp
    * Sell fishes for 1800+ gold and buy Fiberglass Rod and some bait
    * Fish and sell until 80 bait - focused fish bait will be more used than normal
1. Go to the mountain
    * Fish
    * Before passing out keep all the best value items
1. Fishing lv4
### Day 3
1. **GOAL** 45 copper, 5 coal, 25 mine for Furnace and 5 Copper Bar, 2000 Gold for Pick (almost impossible to get it before 16:00 this day buy if possible, do the upgrade today), and fish for money later, if possible keep a Catfish to create Bait
1. Craft Chest and mine rocks for 25 stone if not done yesterday take them, also take all the best value items(including geode)
1. Town: Fish in Town for money
1. Beach: 
    * Sell fish to buy at least material for the furnace and some extra ores to smelt into bars while fishing
    * Forage, should have all or almost for Bait Maker (3 Coral + 1 Sea Urchin)
1. Town: 
    * Fish for more money and sell them to buy the remaining ores to smelt 5 copper bars, do not over sell, also you can keep the fishes in the chest
1. Fishing lv6 (choose Fisher)
### Day 4
1. **GOAL** Make Bait Maker, buy Backpack, Fish for money, plant missing crops
1. Forage
1. Sell fish on Willy's store with Angler's upgraded price and buy bait if needed
1. Buy Backpack, Green Bean, Potato
1. Buy material for Bait Maker and Furnace from Clint and upgrade Pick
1. If raining fish on the River, else on the Lake, if going to the Lake, retrieve the chest from the River fishing area
### Day 5
1. **GOAL** Open Community Center (don't skip the cutscene or you will be brought back to the entrance of the Town)
1. If Copper Pick is ready Mine (check Day 6 guide), else Fish
### Day 6
1. Take Furnace, Axe, take/craft chest leave to the Mines
1. Craft Copper Bars while going down on the Mines
1. If 5 is ready before 14:40, go to Clint for the Axe upgrade
